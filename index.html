Team Introduction: Team Mono is an aspiring group of university students by Agshin Hasanov, Rufat Mammadov, and Yusif Ashurov, who are pursuing their education at ADA University's School of Information Technology and Engineering (SITE). Fueled by enthusiasm for computer science and a commitment to motivate younger generations, they recently arranged an Hour of Code gathering for school students. Through this event, they facilitated an appealing, hands-on experience to pique curiosity in programming among the youngsters. This website is a further step in their development on the journey to become future experts in their own respective fields. Merging their individual skills and making the best out of their SITE education, they undertook the task to make a simple yet elegant website.
Team Mono efforts
Hour of Code:Explore, Play, Create! The Hour of Code, initiated by Code.org, is a global event aimed at making computer science accessible through self-guided tutorials.
Website project: Team Mono from ADA University (SITE1101) created a website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that they learned from Codeacademy.
